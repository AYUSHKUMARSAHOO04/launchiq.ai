LaunchIQ_AI_Product_Foundation.md

Product Name:
LaunchIQ.ai

Category:
AI-powered Product Launch Intelligence Platform

Domain:
D2C Consumer Products

Problem Statement:
D2C brands often struggle to predict customer reactions to pricing, product features, and positioning before launch, leading to uncertainty and failed product decisions.

Solution:
LaunchIQ.ai simulates synthetic consumer reactions using AI-generated personas to help brands test pricing, features, and launch positioning before going to market.

Target Users:
- D2C founders
- Product managers
- Early-stage brands
- Product teams

MVP Objective:
Enable users to simulate consumer feedback for a product before launch.

Input:
- Product name
- Category
- Audience
- Price
- Description
- Features
- Competitor

Output:
- Synthetic personas
- Consumer reactions
- Purchase likelihood
- Price sensitivity
- Feature ranking
- Launch risk score
- Recommendations

Tech Stack:
Frontend:
React + TypeScript + Tailwind

Backend:
FastAPI

Database:
Supabase/PostgreSQL

AI:
Prompt Engineering + NLP

Hosting:
Vercel + Render

Goal:
Build a recruiter-impressive PM portfolio project showcasing product thinking, AI capability, technical depth, and end-to-end execution.
